Biography

I am a computational linguist working as an associate professor at Shanghai Jiao Tong University since 2021. I worked at Japan National Institute of Information and Communications Technology (NICT) from 2016 to 2020. I am curious about the mechanism and intelligence of the NLP, especially MT. I prefer to use simple methods to analyze things deeply.
