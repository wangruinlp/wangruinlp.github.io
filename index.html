Biography

Hi! I am a pre-admitted Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Shanghai Jiao Tong University (expected to graduate in 2024), where I am advised by Prof. Hai Zhao and work in the BCMI-NLP Group . I am expected to obtain my master’s degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in March 2020. Prior to that, in 2016, I received my bachelor’s degree from Wuhan University , advised by Haojun Ai .

From 06/2019 - now, I am an internship research fellow at NICT , working with Masao Utiyama , Rui Wang and Kehai Chen .
