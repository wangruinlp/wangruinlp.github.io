Biography

Dr. Rui Wang is a computational linguist working as an associate professor and he leads the Machine Translation and Language Intelligence Lab (MT-Lab) at Shanghai Jiao Tong University since 2021. Before that, he was a researcher with tenure at Japan National Institute of Information and Communications Technology (NICT) from 2016 to 2020. He has won several first places in top-tier MT/NLP shared tasks, such as WMT-2022/2020/2019/2018, IWSLT-2022, CoNLL-2019, etc. He served as the PC chair of MT Summit 2023 and the area chairs of NeurIPS-2022, ICLR-2023/2022/2021, EMNLP-2022，NAACL-2021, CoNLL-2022/2021, etc.
