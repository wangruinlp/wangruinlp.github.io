Short Bio [Experiences] [Selected Publication] [Full CV Download]

Rui Wang is a tenure-track researcher in NICT. He received his Ph.D. degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2016. His research focuses on machine translation (MT), a classic task in NLP (or even in AI). His main interests are traditional linguistic based and cutting-edge machine learning based approaches for MT.

He (as the first or the corresponding authors) has published more than 30 MT papers in top-tier NLP/AI conferences and journals, such as ACL, EMNLP, ICLR, AAAI, IJCAI, TASLP, etc. He has also won several first places in WMT-2019, WMT-2018, and CoNLL-2019 shared tasks. He served as the area co-chairs of CCL-2018/2019 and the organization co-chairs of PACLIC-29 and YCCL-2012.
